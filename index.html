Welcome to MikeFlora

A family where learning began in 2007. After many years of teaching and nurturing young minds, Mrs. Oshodi envisioned a school built on the strong foundation of family where every child blossoms and grows. Right here in Oba Afunbiowo Estate, Akure, our school was born! We are a place where students learn from textbooks and from a caring community.

We believe learning is most successful when it is filled with joy and encouragement, so our classrooms are alive with creativity, laughter, and discovery.

Learning with Love

Joy-filled classrooms that encourage curiosity, confidence and creativity.

Focus

We partner closely with families, keeping you involved every step of the way.

Growing Together

Balanced development across academics, arts and sports to uncover each child’s gifts.
