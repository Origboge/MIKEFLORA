About MikeFlora

Rooted in family values, dedicated to whole-child growth.

Who We Are

Founded in 2007 in Oba Afunbiowo Estate, Akure, MikeFlora Nursery & Primary School was birthed from a passion to nurture young minds in a safe, loving environment. We believe that the best education is built on partnership with families, academic excellence, and character development.

Our teachers are trained to guide, encourage, and inspire — ensuring each child grows in knowledge, confidence, and resilience.

Our Vision

To raise confident, compassionate, and competent lifelong learners who become responsible leaders in society.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, loving, and innovative learning environment that nurtures academic excellence, creativity, character, and community engagement.

  • Rich academic curriculum with experiential learning
  • Strong moral values and mentorship
  • Active partnership with parents and guardians
