ABOUT US

A family where learning began in 2007, after many years of teaching and nurturing young minds, Mrs. Oshodi decided to create something special. She envisioned a school built on the strong foundation of family and where every child could blossom and grow. And so, right here in Oba Afunbiowo Estate, Akure, our school was born! We weren't just any school; we were a place where students wouldn't just learn from textbooks, but from a caring community. Think of our school as a big, loving family. Mrs. Oshodi, wanted to create a space where every child felt cherished and supported. Our teachers are like caring uncles and aunts, always there to guide you on your learning journey.

We envision a future where every child who walks through our doors becomes a confident, lifelong learner, prepared to contribute positively to their family, community, and the world.

Our mission is to foster a nurturing and inclusive learning environment that celebrates the unique potential of each child. We achieve this by providing a strong academic foundation, promoting creativity and critical thinking skills, and building a strong partnership with families. Since 2007, we've watched countless students blossom into confident and curious individuals. We've been there for their first wobbly steps in education, all the way to their proud graduations. So, if you're looking for a school that feels like a warm embrace, a place where you'll be supported, challenged, and loved then welcome to our family! We can't wait to watch you grow and shine!

Here are a few things that set us apart;

LEARNING WITH LOVE

We believe that learning is most successful when it's filled with joy and encouragement. That's why our classrooms are a buzz with creativity, laughter, and a love for discovery.

FOCUS

We understand the importance of family in a child's life. That's why we work closely with parents and guardians, keeping them involved in their child's education every step of the way.

GROWING TOGETHER

We don't just focus on academics, but on a child's all-around development. We have exciting programs that nurture your talents, be it in music, art, sports, or anything that makes your heart sing!
